Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 14, 2026

Listify ("Service", "App") is committed to protecting your personal information. This document details how we collect, use, and protect your data.

1. What Data Do We Collect?

To provide our services, we may collect and process the following types of data:

  • Contact Information: Name and email address (provided during registration).
  • User Content: The content of your shared lists, tasks, notes, and loyalty cards, which are synchronized in the cloud.
  • Identifiers: User ID and device identifiers to manage your account and synchronization.
  • Diagnostics: Anonymized crash logs and performance data to improve stability.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide, maintain, and improve the Listify service:

  • To authenticate your identity and secure your account.
  • To synchronize your lists and loyalty cards in real-time across all your devices.
  • To enable the "Family" and "Community" sharing features (sharing content with specific users you invite).
  • To provide customer support and troubleshoot technical issues.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not sell your personal data. We partner with trusted third-party providers to operate our infrastructure and services under strict confidentiality agreements. Specifically, we use:

  • Google Firebase: For secure authentication, real-time database hosting, cloud functions, and analytics. Firebase helps us store your data securely and sync it across devices.
  • Facebook Login (Meta) & Sign in with Apple: For social login authentication. When using these services, we may access your name and email address for account creation purposes.

4. Account Deletion & Retention

You have full control over your data. You can initiate the deletion of your account and all associated data directly within the App capabilities (under Settings > Account > Delete Account).

Grace Period Policy: When you initiate account deletion within the app, your account enters a 60-day grace period (suspension). During this time, all your data is retained but is no longer visible to other users. If you log back in within these 60 days, the deletion process is cancelled, and your account is fully restored. This protects you against accidental deletions.

After 60 days of inactivity, your account and all associated data will be permanently deleted from our servers and cannot be recovered.

Alternatively, you may request immediate deletion by contacting us at our support email address.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

support@listify.app
